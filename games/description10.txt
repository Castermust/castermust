System Requirements
Requires a 64-bit processor and operating system
OS: Windows 7 64-bit, Windows 8.1 64-bit or Windows 10 64-bit
Processor: Intel® Core™ i3-2100 @ 3.10 GHz/ AMD FX-4100 @ 3.60 GHz or better
Memory: 4 GB RAM
Graphics: NVIDIA® GeForce® GT 450 1GB/ ATI® Radeon™ HD 7770 1 GB or better
DirectX: Version 11
Storage: 110 GB available space
Sound Card: Directx 9.0x
Additional Notes: Dual-analog Gamepad recommended. Initial installation requires one-time internet connection for Steam authentication; software installations required (included with the game) include DirectX and Visual C++ Redistributable 2012. In order to play NBA 2K23 on PC, you need a processor capable of supporting SSE 4.2 and AVX.
